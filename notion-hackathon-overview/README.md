# Hackathon-Related Notion Pages — Overview (English)

This is a complete index of every hackathon-related page found in the Notion workspace, with short English summaries. Original content is mostly in German.

The workspace contains **two distinct hackathons**:

1. **AI Hackathon 2026** — part of the larger Future Leader Summit (FLS) 2026 in Hamburg. Organized by ChefTreff.
2. **Terns — HackPune 2026** — a separate 24-hour AI hackathon planned for Pune, India.

Most of the wiki database (`Wiki - FUTURE LEADER SUMMIT 2026`) is the partner/exhibitor/speaker handbook for FLS 2026 — the umbrella event that hosts the AI Hackathon. Pages covering tickets, partners, speakers, logistics, etc. are listed here because they directly affect anyone running or attending the hackathon.
