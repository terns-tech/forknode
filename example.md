How It Works:
9 challenges, 6 teams per challenge, 4–6 people per team. You don’t need to be a developer – we need product thinkers, designers, and strategists too. Apply now and join us!

Apply - Submit your application via our form. Solo or with a team.
Get accepted - We review all applications and get back to you within two weeks.
Confirm your spot - Secure your place by completing a short confirmation form within 48h - or your spot goes to the next in line.
Get matched - We assign you to a challenge and team based on your skills and preferences.
Build - 24 hours to go from problem to prototype. With mentorship from the challenge partners.
Pitch - Present your solution on the main stage. Judges pick the winners.
